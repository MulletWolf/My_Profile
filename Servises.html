1-2-1 Personal Training

Mysterra offers 1-1/2-1 sessions with our personal trainers in our private gyms. Coaching plans are based on your needs and current goals. Calcuate your progress through us.

How does our personal training work

  1. Consultation

    Before the first session, we go over the services we cover in our gyms. Here, we give you a tour where we explain the equipment. Before this your personal trainer will ask you questions on your health and fitness goals, your barriers to sucess,your short term goals,your experience, what you typically eat and what you want us to do for you. We ask these questions to figure out a plan to combat your obstacles and to tell you how you can do that. We gather basic information such as your name, height, width, body fat percentage, measurements and more.

  2. Intake and Assessment

    During your first session, we take your measurements,your goals and personal preferences(just incase you changed your mind) . We test your body control and indentify under or over-active muscle groups. You will do physical tests such as a 3 minute step test, 1 minute calorie burn on Air Dyne, push ups(reps to failure) and testing your maximal plank time. This is where we show clients that they are making positive changes over time to keep you motivated.

  3. Nutrition Counselling

    We teach you how to nourish your body the right way with with basic foods in the correct amounts. A customized meal plan written by registered dietitians is also possible free of charge.

  4. Exclusive access to Mysterra app

    Our online platform provides you all the additional tools you need to progress- from recipe books, to follow along mobility and yoga videos. We got your back every step of the way.

  5. Weekly check-ins

    We check weekly how you are doing both mentally and physically and if your goals are still the same.We make adjustments when needed.Your progression is our top priority.Here, we address your motivaton, discipline and mental state.
